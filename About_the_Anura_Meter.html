About the Anura Meter

Anura Meter tracks selected manifesto promises of President Anura Kumara Dissanayake based on their alignment with key areas of public interest, relating to economic reform, governance, anti-corruption, and social protection.

  • Economic reform-related promises focus on investment, trade, public finance, and tax reforms.
  • Governance-related promises address processes and systems of decision-making, coordination, and management within the government for better transparency and accountability.
  • Anti-corruption-related promises focus on implementing systems and legal frameworks to prevent, detect, and punish corrupt practices within public institutions and government operations.
  • Social protection-related promises refer to policies, programs, and regulatory mechanisms aimed at reducing poverty, vulnerability, and discrimination.

Tracking will rely on:

  • Publicly available information from official government sources
  • Government responses to Right to Information (RTI) requests
  • Crowd-sourced updates, verified by the Parliamentary Research Team at Verit√© Research

The 24 promises that were selected were based on our observations on the most critical elements of the manifesto that generated high public interest.
